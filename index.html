Read More About Dr. Mahathi

Mahathi developed an interest in traditional medicine during childhood, often questioning her grandmother about the medicinal properties of herbs and their mechanisms of action. She pursued formal training in Ayurveda and graduated as a gold medalist.

Since beginning clinical practice, Mahathi has treated over 500 patients using personalized Ayurvedic protocols. Her approach integrates classical treatments such as Panchakarma with individualized dietary modifications and lifestyle interventions. She has also incorporated music therapy into her practice as a complementary healing modality.

In addition to clinical work, she mentors emerging practitioners in Ayurvedic medicine. Her treatment philosophy emphasizes identifying root causes rather than symptom management, and fostering patient agency in the healing process.
